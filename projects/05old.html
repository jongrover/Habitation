福隆淨灘裝置藝術節
Title: Habitation No.5, 海怪（Sea Monster）
聯合福隆地區長期關懷環境與教育的黃吉元先生、福隆國小、以及地方人士共同發起的淨灘裝置藝術節。預計2017年5月為第一屆活動試辦，由藝術家蔡慧盈帶領學生與民眾以工作坊形式進行，5/21裝置完成。
活動主旨以藝術創作為中心，帶領社區居民共同發展對自身環境的關注，並透過個人的實際參與建立社群向心力，同時達到宣傳環保意識、愛護自然的教育目標。
長遠目標：從福隆社區出發，將此活動推廣到鄰近社區，發展成在地長期、有組織性的固定特色活動。預期大型淨灘裝置藝術節可帶動福隆地區經濟活動，帶給社區正面形象，提醒也鼓勵觀光客遊玩的同時也從自己出發，時時隨手做環保，永續經營。期望發展成為年度藝術節，或是短期駐村形式，邀請國內外藝術家帶領居民共同淨灘與創作，讓藝術結合生活，傳達環保觀念。

The first edition of Fulong Beach Coastal Cleanup Art Project is led by artist, Hui-Ying Tsai, and Ji-Yuan Huang, the founder of Being Surfing School in collaboration with Fulong Elementary School. Sea Monster is an art project that focuses on raising awareness of ocean plastic pollution and the environmental-friendly community building. The execution of the piece was through free workshops opened to the public and art classes at Fulong Elementary School. The piece is made with 100% coastal garbage gathered from 3 coastal cleanup events by volunteers and students at Fulong Elementary School.
The design of the piece is a mysterious beached marine mammal that is half buried in the sand. The body of the Sea Monster is a found styrofoam boat and its fins are made with various plastic garbage. The majority of the materials are put together with found ropes and fishing lines on the beach with minimal reinforcement of glue if necessary in order to keep the waste of production to its minimum. The artwork was built with help of a group of volunteers in total 5 days. The piece will be taken apart and sent to garbage and recycling center before the next typhoon hits the area.
The long-term goal of this project is to make it into an annual event/art residency and progressively develop Fulong to be an active community that demonstrates care for the costal environment.
