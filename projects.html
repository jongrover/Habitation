Works About Contact

Home is a verb. The living beings set up the boundary to push outward, and the line is pushed in constantly, which suggests that a sense of home must be always in flux and that living beings must insist on working toward the ownership.

『家』是一個動詞。生命設定邊界向外推展，而邊界則不斷地被周圍環境向內推。此邊界具有不斷流動的特性，而生命必須不間斷地持續努力經營『家』這個概念，以行動去擁有。
